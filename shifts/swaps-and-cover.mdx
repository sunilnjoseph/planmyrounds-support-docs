After a roster is published, changes happen through three flows: **swaps**, **cover requests**, and **admin reassignment**. All three keep a full audit trail.

## Swaps

A swap is when two staff members exchange shifts directly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Initiate">
    The requester opens one of their shifts and taps **Request swap**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a counterpart">
    They choose the colleague's shift to swap with. Only eligible shifts are listed (matching role, no conflicts).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Counterparty approves">
    The other person receives a notification and accepts or declines.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Admin signs off">
    If swap approval is required (set in Org Settings), an admin gives final sign-off.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Shifts switch">
    Both rosters update and both users are notified.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Cover requests

A cover request offers a single shift to colleagues — useful when no direct swap is needed.

1. Staff member opens a shift → **Request cover**.
2. Adds an optional reason and chooses who can see it (whole team, role-matched only, etc.).
3. Eligible colleagues are notified and can **Accept**.
4. First accepter takes the shift (or admin chooses if multiple).
5. The original assignee is released from the shift.

<Note>
  Cover does **not** automatically log leave. If the requester needs the time off recorded, they should also submit a [leave request](/leave-management/overview).
</Note>

## Admin reassignment

Admins can reassign any shift directly without a swap or cover flow — used for sickness, emergencies, or corrections.

- Open the shift → change assignee → add a reason.
- Both the previous and new assignee are notified.
- The change is recorded in the shift's history.

## Approval rules

Configure in **Organisation Settings → Shifts**:

| Rule | Options |
| --- | --- |
| Swap approval | Auto-approve · Admin approval required |
| Cover approval | First-come · Admin chooses · Manager assigns |
| Notice period | e.g. no swaps within 24h of shift start |
| Cross-role swaps | Allowed · Blocked |

## Audit trail

Every shift has a **History** tab showing:

- Who created it
- Each reassignment, with reason and timestamp
- Swap / cover requests, including declines
- Admin overrides

This is your evidence trail for compliance and dispute resolution.
