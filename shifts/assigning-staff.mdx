Once shifts exist on a roster, you assign them to staff. A shift can be assigned, reassigned, or left **unassigned** (a visible gap).

## Assign a single shift

1. Click the shift in the roster grid.
2. In the side panel, choose an assignee from the dropdown.
3. PlanMyRounds shows only **eligible** staff — i.e. users with the required role and no conflicts.
4. Click **Save**.

<Tip>
  Hold <kbd>Shift</kbd> while dragging a shift between cells to **copy** rather than move it.
</Tip>

## Bulk assign

For filling many gaps at once:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Filter">
    Use the roster filter to show **Unassigned only**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select">
    Multi-select shifts (click \+ shift-click, or **Select all visible**).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Auto-assign or pick">
    Choose **Auto-assign** to let PlanMyRounds distribute fairly, or pick a single user to take all selected shifts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Auto-assign rules

Auto-assign respects, in priority order:

1. Role eligibility
2. Approved leave and existing shifts
3. Minimum rest hours
4. Contracted hours / FTE balance
5. Fair distribution of antisocial shifts (nights, weekends)

<Note>
  Auto-assign is a _suggestion_ — you can review and adjust before publishing.
</Note>

## Handling conflicts

When a conflict is detected, the shift is highlighted in **amber** (warning) or **red** (block):

| Colour | Meaning | Action |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 🟡 Amber | Soft warning (e.g. close to rest limit) | Review; can save with reason |
| 🔴 Red | Hard conflict (overlap, leave, role mismatch) | Must resolve or override |

Click the badge on the shift to see the specific reason.

## Reassign or unassign

- **Reassign**: open the shift and change the assignee. The original assignee is notified if the roster is published.
- **Unassign**: open the shift and clear the assignee. The shift becomes a visible gap.

## Tracking workload

The **Workload** view (top of the roster) shows per-person totals for the period:

- Total hours
- Day / night / weekend split
- Contracted vs. assigned hours
- Leave taken

Use this to balance the roster before publishing.
