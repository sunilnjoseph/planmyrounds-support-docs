**PlanMyRounds** is rostering software for clinical teams — hospitalist groups, on-call rotations, and any team that runs shift work. It replaces spreadsheets and WhatsApp chains with a single source of truth for who is working, when, where, and on what.

This documentation covers every critical flow: building rosters, managing leave, handling swaps and cover, and administering your organisation.
